Forecast Methodology

Linear Regression

Forecast is calculated from Prometheus historical metrics using daily average aggregation, then projected forward with a linear regression trend line. Projected utilization is compared against resource capacity to estimate warning and critical risk windows.

Data Source

Prometheus Range Query

CPU, memory, storage, and pod metrics are sampled from Prometheus history.

Aggregation

Daily Average

Raw range data is grouped into daily average points before trend calculation.

Projection

y = slope × day + intercept

The trend slope is extended based on the selected forecast horizon.

Risk Threshold

Warning 75% · Critical 90%

ETA is calculated from current usage, growth slope, and capacity threshold.
